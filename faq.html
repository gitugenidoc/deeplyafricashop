FAQ

What is included?

Country story, AFCON and World Cup summaries, iconic moments, player names, quiz, digital badge, poster and wallpaper placeholders, share text, and a future merch-code placeholder.

Is the result a lottery?

No. The country assignment changes content theme only. Every pass delivers the same product type and value.

How is my link delivered?

The backend receives the paid Shopify order webhook, creates a secure token, stores the assignment, and emails the reveal link after email delivery is configured.
